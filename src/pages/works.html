Works

Four browser-native works by Mozare. Each gives reading a different condition and asks the browser to carry it in another way.

01

The Black Bird

Hypergraph research poem · 2026

A black bird appears beside a body. From that contact, the work opens a field where source-bearing scenes, named objects, citations, and reader routes remain available to one another. Reading moves between the graph and the Reader, following how each contact alters the field.

02

Winter Road

Digital haiga space · 2026

Nine haiku inhabit a dark field that withholds them from a single view. The reader searches by moving, holds one poem briefly, and feels the surrounding darkness change as another begins to appear.

03

Grave-Machine

Generative e-poem · 2026

Material from the play Grave returns as a running procedure. Bodies, documents, rooms, and administrative actions recur in changing arrangements while the trace of each line remains visible beside the generated surface.

04

TAROKE RIMIXER

Work for generative literature · 2026

TAROKE RIMIXER makes generative composition itself available as an encounter. A writer shapes material, grammar, recurrence, and pacing, then reads the runtime as evidence for what the work should keep, alter, or refuse.
