TAROKE RIMIXER

TAROKE RIMIXER turns the making of a generative poem into a visible, revisable encounter. The writer brings the material, composes the grammar through which it may change, and shapes the time of its recurrence. A generated line can be tested without losing contact with the construction that produced it.

CONTEXT

The poem and the means of making it

The work continues the literary proposition made public by Taroko Gorge: a compact generator can be read, altered, and carried into another ground. TAROKE RIMIXER expands that proposition into a full compositional environment.

The writer is not offered a blank box followed by an opaque result. Material is named. Transformations are local. Line-making devices can be inspected. Stanza and flow determine how the poem changes over time. The completed project can leave the workbench as its own playable artifact.

Grave-Machine was made through these layers, but it remains one authored work rather than a default template.
