The Black Bird Field

Browser-native literary works by Mozare, shaped through research and generative writing.

The Black Bird Field is the online home of four works by Mozare, the artistic name of Mohammad Zare. In The Black Bird, reading crosses a graph of linked scenes and objects. Winter Road yields to proximity. Grave-Machine keeps dramatic language moving beside the trace of its generation. TAROKE RIMIXER turns the construction of a poem into an encounter of its own.

The Black Bird

A black bird appears beside a body, and other scenes begin to gather around that contact. The graph gives each return a place. A research note can be opened and left; a mapping note condenses a charged relation; a name carries its source language into the field. Reading develops through the crossings between them and the route the reader leaves behind.

Winter Road

Nine haiku occupy a dark field, but none is offered as a page. The reader searches by moving. A poem brightens at close range, can be kept briefly, then recedes as attention travels elsewhere. The unlit distance between poems becomes part of what is read.

Generative e-poem · 2026

Grave-Machine

An Iranian Remix of Taroko Gorge

Grave-Machine carries material from the play Grave into the remix lineage of Taroko Gorge. Bodies and documents pass through the same recurring procedures, while the generated poem remains beside the trace that produced it. The work does not retell the play. It keeps its pressure in motion.

Work for generative literature · 2026

TAROKE RIMIXER

TAROKE RIMIXER turns the making of a generative poem into a visible, revisable encounter. A writer brings the material, composes the grammar through which it can change, and sets the rhythms of recurrence. Running the work is part of writing it: each produced line becomes evidence for what should remain, be altered, or be refused.

02 / PRACTICE

The form is discovered through the material

The practice does not separate research from writing and then place interface at the end. A source can become a scene inside a graph. A short poem can depend on darkness and movement. Dramatic language can be recast as a procedure. A compositional work can become the place where another poem finds its form.

01

Research that can return

Speculative research poetry keeps the condition of a source visible while allowing it to enter another arrangement. The work can answer a source without pretending to verify it.

02

Writing through constraints

Generative writing places authorship in the rules of recurrence. Material is chosen, possible changes are shaped, and time becomes part of composition.

03

The interface carries consequence

An interface decides when something can be seen, how long it remains, and what the reader can carry forward. It participates in the writing rather than delivering it from outside.
